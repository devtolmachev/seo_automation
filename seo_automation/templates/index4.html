01

Schedule an Appointment

Once you have set up your account, you can easily schedule an appointment with your doctor or healthcare professional. You will be able to select the type of appointment you would like, the date and time of the appointment, and any additional services that may be required.

02

Connecting with a Doctor

Our website is designed to provide a secure connection between you and your doctor so that you can receive quality care. You will be able to view your appointment details and access any additional services that have been requested.

03

Attending Your Appointment

When it is time for your appointment, you will be able to connect with your doctor or healthcare professional securely from anywhere. Depending on the type of appointment, you may be able to see, hear, and talk to your doctor or healthcare team via video or audio chat.

04

After Your Appointment

You should receive a follow-up message from your doctor or healthcare professional. You can also access additional information about your appointment, such as any follow-up instructions or medications that have been prescribed, in your dashboard. You can also access your medical records, lab results, and other information.
